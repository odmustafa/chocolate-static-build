Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Chocolate & Art Show Dallas

Chocolate & Art Show is an immersive experience combining live art creation, music performances, body art, and artisan chocolate tasting. It's a unique cultural event that brings together local artists, musicians, and chocolatiers for two unforgettable nights.

The event takes place on September 18-19, 2025, at Lofty Spaces in Dallas, TX. Doors open at 7:00 PM both nights, with the last entry at 12:30 AM.

Yes, this is a 21+ event. Valid government-issued photo ID is required for entry. No exceptions.

Ticket prices vary by night and availability. Visit our tickets page for current pricing and to purchase tickets through Eventbrite.

You'll experience live art creation, interactive installations, body art performances, live music and DJ sets, artisan chocolate tastings, and an immersive atmosphere that blends all these elements together.

Due to limited capacity, we strongly recommend purchasing tickets in advance. Door sales are not guaranteed and depend on availability.

Yes, there is parking available at the venue. We also recommend rideshare services for convenience. See our contact page for detailed directions and parking information.

Personal photography is welcome! We encourage sharing your experience on social media. Professional photography equipment may require prior approval.

Visit our artists page to apply as an artist, vendor, or musician. Space is limited and applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.

For additional questions, please email us at info@chocolateandartshow.com or visit our contact page.
